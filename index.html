Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our methodology and classes.

What is the duration of the tutorial and how is it structured? +

The program is divided into two comprehensive 4-month phases: Phase One: August 31st to December 27th. Phase Two: January 11th to April 30th
The entire JAMB and WAEC syllabus for Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry is completed within each phase. This means whether you join in Phase One or Phase Two, you will receive a complete, start-to-finish walkthrough of all topics.

How are the live classes conducted? +

Our regular interactive live classes are held on Telegram, allowing for easy access, low data consumption, and continuous engagement. In addition, we host a special live class every week via Zoom to tackle advanced topics, solve questions in real-time, and host visual masterclasses.

What if I miss a live class? +

You don’t have to worry about missing out. All our Telegram sessions remain in the private group chat, so you can easily scroll up and read through the lesson at your convenience. For our weekly Zoom sessions, we provide recorded video links and summarized notes to all registered students after the class ends.

Are the mock tests standard for JAMB and WAEC? +

Absolutely. Our mock exams are carefully curated using thousands of past questions and standard marking guides to simulate the exact difficulty, timing, and interface of the actual high-stakes exams.

Do I need a laptop to join the classes? +

Not at all! Just ensure you have the Telegram and Zoom applications downloaded and installed on your device.

Can I ask questions during lessons? +

Yes! Our classes are highly interactive. During our Telegram sessions, there are dedicated Q&A segments where you can drop your questions. Furthermore, our weekly Zoom classes give you the opportunity to unmute yourself and speak directly with the tutors for personalized clarity.

How do I register and join the Nova Spark classes? +

Registration is very simple! Just click the "Enroll Now" or "Sign Up" button on our website, complete your registration, and make your payment. Once confirmed, you will immediately receive an exclusive invite link to join our private Telegram classroom and start learning.
